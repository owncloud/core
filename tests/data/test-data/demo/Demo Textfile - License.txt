ownCloud – web services under your control

* Store your files centrally, encrypted, on hardware controlled by you, with automatic backup & versioning
* Work on- and offline synchronize your desktop apps or use online media player, photo gallery, feed reader etc.
* Access everywhere it’s a web application you can reach easily with any device
* Share files with your friends and coworkers

http://ownCloud.org
